Developed by Roni Guz and Hodaya Takele

Welcome to the best version of the Pac-Man game!

The Sesame Street gang is having a party and the cookie monster is on the run trying to eat as many cookies as possible. Elmo and other friends are trying to stop him before he eats everything. If you want to be a winner and not a sore loser you need to help the Cookie-Man escape from his friends, eat cookies and gain at least 100 points before the time stop.
Good Luck!

Uses: We used input picker In the sign up and setting page. In addition we used console log in order to deabug our code when we had a bug we could not understad. We used RegEx and jQuery to validate our signUp inputs.

Our main struggles: Designing the site took us a lot of time. Understanding the right flow to manage the different content sections, Implementing the logic, and making it work. We also had a problem with the game board, timing, and scaling of the different objects to fit on the board.
